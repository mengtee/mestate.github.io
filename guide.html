Guide for housing

  • Know your budget
    • Knowing your budget before purchasing your property. For the first time buyer, it is advisable to set your set your limit in purchasing your first house, avoid puchase a houses that is at the border line of your budget, it might get you suffer if you still wanted to purchase second or third property. Do take into consideration the budget for house furnishing as well as monthly maintenance fees.
  • Know your location
    • Purchasing a house that near your working place is very important if you don't want to travel far. Do take into consideration that a property value also affected by its own location. It is best to purchase a house that have a high resell value yet near your working place/ outher accessibility. A property that is Transit Oriented Development will also increase the future property value.
  • Know your priority
    • That is no perfect property that meet your criteria, perhaps sometime you have to choose a property that is the best fit for you but not perfect! Make sure to purchase a property that tick most your boxes.

Good luck in finding one!
